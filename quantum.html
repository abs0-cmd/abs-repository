What is Quantum Computing?

By TechPulse Team • May 8, 2025 • 6 min read

Quantum computing is a rapidly-emerging technology that harnesses the laws of quantum mechanics to solve problems too complex for classical computers.

How It Works

Unlike regular computers that use bits (0s and 1s) to process data, quantum computers use qubits. Qubits can exist in a state of superposition, allowing them to calculate multiple possibilities at the exact same time.
